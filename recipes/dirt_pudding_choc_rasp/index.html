Chocolate and Raspberries Dirt Pudding

 1 hungry backpacker  5 min prep, 1 hour waiting time  73.8g / serving

Ingredients

Note: This recipe contains affiliate links. You can read my disclosure here.

  • ¼ cup (60 ml) freeze dried raspberries
  • 2 Oreo cookies, crushed
  • 3 tbsp. (45 ml) instant chocolate pudding mix
  • 3 tbsp. (45 ml) powdered skim milk

Equipment

  • Bowl to eat (you could always eat directly in the bag, no judgement here!)
  • Spork or spoon

Before trip

  1. Mix in plastic bag #1: raspberries and Oreo cookies.
  2. Mix in plastic bag #2: pudding mix and powdered skim milk

At camp

  1. Add ½ cup (125 ml) of cold water to bag # 2.
  2. Mix well by shaking the bag for a few minutes.
  3. Wait for at least 1 hour for the mix to thicken up.
  4. Add the raspberries and Oreo cookies. Enjoy!
