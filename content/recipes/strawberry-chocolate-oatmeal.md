## Equipment
- Stove + fuel
- Pot to boil water
- Bowls to eat
- sporks or forks!

## Before trip
1. Mix in a plastic bag: all the ingredients
 
## At camp
1. Bring to boil about 1 cup of water.
1. Pour boiling water over dry ingredients and mix.
1. Wait for a few minutes and enjoy!
